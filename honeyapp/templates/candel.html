Coconut product Articles

Desiccated coconut

Uses: Desiccated coconut (DC) is dehydrated coconut meat that is finely grated or shredded. It is produced widely in India and primarily used by the confectionery industry and other food sectors.
Medicinal Benefits: DC also serves as an alternative to fresh grated coconut in many household recipes. The product is available in various grades, determined by the fineness of the coconut material.

Coconut Powder

Uses:Spray drying is an effective method for preserving coconut milk. The resulting spray-dried coconut milk powder can be easily reconstituted into liquid form by adding water, making it suitable for various culinary uses.
Medicinal Benefits: This product offers advantages such as reduced storage requirements, cost-efficient bulk packaging, and a longer shelf life

Coconut Milk

Uses: Coconut milk is an emulsion of oil, protein, and water obtained by pressing fresh grated coconut. It comes in various forms: undiluted (coconut milk), diluted, and concentrated (coconut cream).
Medicinal Benefits: Extracted from fresh coconut, it can be used directly or diluted for a wide range of dishes, including fish, meat, curries, desserts, and baked goods.
